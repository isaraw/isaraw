An innovative forward thinking communication expert, digital marketing professional and graphic designer with experience in developing and designing creative and cost effective marketing strategies for Fortune 1000 corporations.

Since 2006, I have worked in media, digital and publishing companies. These experiences have provided me with a broad and long-standing range of skills, including digital marketing, social media management, website and CMS management, email marketing, PPC , SEO and SEM, Google Analytics, Google Webmaster, news and content writing, publishing and researching, proofreading and editing of promotional materials. I'm also interested in art, culture and lifestyle journalism, travel writing and journalism concering social issue such as migration, unprivileged people, poverty, discrimination, welfare policies or disabilities. I'm interested in international and global politics.
