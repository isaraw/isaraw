Adrien Grimmeau: Graffiti in Brussels
(Ragazine.cc, May 2011)

Neerpede Park is a huge open air art gallery. Little known by the people of Brussels and surely not included in any tour guide or do-it-yourself journey in Belgium, it lies on the outskirts of Brussels, Eddie Mercks metro stop. The pillars of the three flyovers that cross the park have become the favourite spot of Brussels’ graffiti artists to show dozens and dozens of works of street art.
